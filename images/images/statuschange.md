# statuschange

Update the status of parcels and machines to maintain real-time tracking for the back office. This feature allows you to move items through collection and delivery workflows efficiently. You will achieve full visibility and process continuity by accurately recording each stage of the logistics cycle,.

### Getting Started

Prerequisites:
*   Active dispatch user login credentials,.
*   Access to the device's camera for barcode scanning,.

1. Tap on **Scan**,.

![Frame at 0:11](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_11.png "Scan button – Tap here to start the barcode scanning process.")

2. Open the **Barcode scanner** and scan the item,.

![Flow 0:14 to 0:18](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_14_to_0_to_18.gif "Barcode scanner – Point the scanner at the barcode to capture the data.")

3. Tap the **Tick mark** to log in as a dispatch user,.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_21.png "Tick mark – Finalizes the scan and identifies you as the dispatcher.")

### Feature Overview

*   **DA is collected**: Toggle this switch to record that a parcel has been collected.

![Frame at 0:41](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_41.png "DA is collected toggle – Sets the status to collected in the system.")

*   **Delivered**: Toggle this switch to mark an item as successfully delivered,.

![Frame at 3:00](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_3_to_00.png "Delivered toggle – Sets the status to delivered for the back office.")

*   **Reserve name**: Enter the name of the recipient or the person handling the item,.

![Frame at 1:24](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_24.png "Reserve name field – Captures the name for delivery records.")

*   **Shuttle**: Select the specific shuttle or timing for the delivery reservation,,.

![Frame at 1:48](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_48.png "Shuttle selection – Assigns the item to the correct transport unit.")

### How To: Update Status to Collected

1. Toggle the **DA is collected** option.

![Frame at 0:41](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_41.png "Status toggle – Activating the collection status.")

2. Select the appropriate **Pattern**,.

![Frame at 0:56](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_0_to_56.png "Pattern selection – Choosing the item pattern from the list.")

3. Tap the **Right arrow**,.

![Frame at 1:08](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_08.png "Right arrow – Moves the process to the next step.")

4. Take a snap of the parcel,.

![Flow 1:10 to 1:13](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_10_to_1_to_13.gif "Camera action – Taking a photo for evidence of collection.")

5. Tap the **Right arrow**,.

6. Enter the **Reserve name**,.

![Frame at 1:24](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_24.png "Name field – Typing the name of the person involved.")

7. Tap the **Right arrow** to set the **Shuttle** selection timing,.

![Frame at 1:48](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_48.png "Shuttle screen – Finalizing timing details.")

8. Tap the **Right arrow** and then the **Tick mark**,.

9. Tap **Confirm** on the validation pop-up,.

![Flow 1:55 to 2:01](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_1_to_55_to_2_to_01.gif "Validation pop-up – Confirming the status update for the back office.")

### How To: Update Status to Delivered

1. Scan the same machine and tap the **Tick mark**,.

2. Toggle the **Delivered** status,.

![Frame at 3:00](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_3_to_00.png "Status toggle – Switching the item to delivered status.")

3. Toggle the **Confirm** button,.

4. Take a snap of the item,.

5. Tap the **Tick mark**,.

6. Enter the **Reserve name** and tap the **Right arrow**,.

7. Select the **Shuttle** and tap the **Right arrow**,.

8. Obtain the signature of the recipient on the screen,.

![Frame at 3:45](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_3_to_45.png "Signature pad – Capturing the recipient's digital signature.")

9. Tap the **Tick mark**,.

10. Tap **Confirm** on the validation pop-up,,.

![Frame at 4:01](../images/statuschange-statuschange_timestamp_4_to_01.png "Confirm button – Finalizing the delivery in the system.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Dispatcher Privileges**: Logging in as a dispatcher allows you to change multiple statuses like collected and delivered,.
*   💡 **Workflow Automation**: Completing a status change automatically triggers the next step in the delivery workflow,.
*   ⚠️ **Login Required**: You must be logged in as a dispatch user to ensure status changes sync with the back office,.
