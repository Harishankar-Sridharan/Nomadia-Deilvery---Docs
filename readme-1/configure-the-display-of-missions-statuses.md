# Configure the display of mission’s statuses

This feature allows users to customize the mission statuses displayed on their home page dashboard. By selecting up to five statuses, users can tailor the view to show the most relevant information Based on their operational needs, helping them stay focused and efficient.

From the Home page,

1. Click the Account icon located at the top-right corner of the screen.
2. From the dropdown menu, select My Preferences.
3. Choose up to five statuses of the Mission Stats on the Home page to display from the following options: Waiting, Not Received, Not Delivered, To Be Delivered and Loaded
4. After selecting the desired statuses, click on Save.

![](../.gitbook/assets/image-7.png)

5. Your home page will display the selected KPI’s.
