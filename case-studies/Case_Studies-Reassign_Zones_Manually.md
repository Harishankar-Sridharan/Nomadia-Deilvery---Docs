# Case-Studies

Manual zone reassignment allows you to move missions to a new territory layout on your own terms. Use this when switching between different studies or seasonal configurations where automatic triggers do not apply. You will achieve accurate mission records and uninterrupted planning cycles.

### Getting Started

*   At least two studies configured for the same area.
*   A set of active missions needing reassignment.

1. Navigate to the **Configuration** module.

![Frame at 2:20](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_20.png "Configuration module – Accessing the system settings.")

2. Click on **Manage studies and zones**.

![Frame at 2:23](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_23.png "Manage studies and zones – Opening the zone configuration list.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Enable toggle**: Activates or deactivates a specific study configuration.

![Frame at 2:33](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_33.png "Enable toggle – Switching the study status.")

*   **Saved filter**: Isolates specific missions based on predefined criteria like old zone assignments.

![Frame at 3:22](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_22.png "Saved filter – Selecting targeted missions.")

*   **Reassign zone**: Instantly updates selected missions to reflect the currently active study geometry.

![Frame at 3:44](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_44.png "Reassign zone – Button to trigger the manual update.")

### How To: Switch Mission Studies

1. Find the current active study and click the **Edit** button.

![Frame at 2:28](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_28.png "Edit button – Opening study settings.")

2. Deactivate the **Enable toggle** and click **Save**.

![Flow 2:30 to 2:36](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_30_to_2_to_36.gif "Enable toggle – Deactivating the current study.")

3. Find the new experimental study and click **Edit**.

![Frame at 2:44](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_44.png "Edit button – Opening the new study.")

4. Switch the **Enable toggle** to active and click **Save**.

![Flow 2:46 to 2:52](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_2_to_46_to_2_to_52.gif "Enable toggle – Activating the new study.")

5. Head to the **Missions** tab.

![Frame at 3:10](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_10.png "Missions tab – Accessing the mission table.")

6. Apply a **Filter** to isolate missions assigned to the old study.

![Flow 3:17 to 3:24](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_17_to_3_to_24.gif "Filter – Isolating the missions to be moved.")

7. Select all relevant missions in the table.

![Frame at 3:39](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_39.png "Selection box – Choosing missions for update.")

8. Open the **Actions** menu and click **Reassign zone**.

![Flow 3:41 to 3:44](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_41_to_3_to_44.gif "Actions menu – Selecting the reassignment command.")

9. Click **Yes** to confirm the reassignment.

![Frame at 3:48](../images/Case-Studies-Reassign_Zones_Manually_timestamp_3_to_48.png "Confirmation prompt – Finalizing the zone switch.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Safe Testing**: Test new layouts by reassigning a sample of missions before committing the whole fleet.
*   💡 **Seasonal Transitions**: Use manual reassignment to switch between summer and winter configurations instantly.
*   ⚠️ **Automation Limits**: The system only auto-updates for geometry changes, not for new study activations.
*   ⚠️ **Human Oversight**: Always review the filtered list to ensure only the intended missions move.
