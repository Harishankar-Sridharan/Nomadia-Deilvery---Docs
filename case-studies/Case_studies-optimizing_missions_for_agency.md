# Case-Studies

Nomadia Delivery automates the process from mission selection to optimized route creation. This feature reduces fuel costs and CO2 emissions by eliminating inefficient sequencing. Planners achieve achievable routes for drivers with minimal manual intervention.

### Getting Started

*   Zones must be built and sectors assigned.
*   Deliverers must be mapped and configured in the system.
*   Missions must be geocoded and loaded.

1. Navigate to the **Mission tab**.

![Frame at 1:22](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_22.png "Mission tab – The starting point where missions are loaded.")

2. Click the **Action menu**.

![Frame at 1:35](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_35.png "Action menu – Opens available operations for the mission list.")

3. Select **Automatic optimization by agencies**.

![Flow 1:35 to 1:46](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_35_to_1_to_46.gif "Action menu – Selecting the optimization wizard to begin the process.")

### Feature Overview

*   **From date** and **To date**: Define the specific time window for missions.

![Frame at 1:53](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_53.png "Date fields – Selecting the start and end dates for mission selection.")

*   **Agency** selection: Choose the specific agency to optimize.

![Frame at 2:20](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_2_to_20.png "Agency dropdown – Picking the target agency for the run.")

*   **Automatically validate the routes**: Toggle this to finalize routes without manual review.

![Frame at 3:25](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_3_to_25.png "Automatic validation toggle – Enables hands-off route finalization.")

*   **Minimum validation threshold**: Set a quality gate between 50 and 100 percent.

![Frame at 3:49](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_3_to_49.png "Threshold field – Determining the required success rate for auto-validation.")

*   **Publish routes**: Instantly pushes validated routes to the driver's mobile application.

![Frame at 4:37](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_4_to_37.png "Publish routes toggle – Sends routes directly to driver devices.")

*   **Progress bar**: Displays real-time status for each team's optimization.

![Frame at 6:43](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_6_to_43.png "Progress bar – Monitoring the live status of the optimization run.")

### How To: Launch Optimization

1. Click the **Action menu**.

![Frame at 1:35](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_35.png "Action menu – Accessing the optimization tools.")

2. Select **Automatic optimization by agencies**.

![Frame at 1:41](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_41.png "Wizard launch – Opening the step-by-step optimization wizard.")

3. Set the **From date** and **To date** for your missions.

![Frame at 1:53](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_1_to_53.png "Date selection – Defining the mission window.")

4. Select your agency from the **Agency** list.

![Frame at 2:20](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_2_to_20.png "Agency selection – Choosing the relevant agency.")

5. Choose to include or exclude missions in **waiting status**.

![Frame at 2:31](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_2_to_31.png "Waiting status – Deciding if pending missions should be optimized.")

6. Click **Next**.

![Frame at 3:04](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_3_to_04.png "Next button – Proceeding to validation settings.")

### How To: Configure Validation and Notifications

1. Toggle **Automatically validate the routes** for high-volume operations.

![Frame at 3:25](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_3_to_25.png "Validation toggle – Enabling automatic route finalization.")

2. Set a **Minimum validation threshold** as a quality gate.

![Frame at 3:49](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_3_to_49.png "Validation threshold – Ensuring only high-quality routes are auto-validated.")

3. Enable **Ignore conflicts** to override existing data with the new plan.

![Frame at 4:19](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_4_to_19.png "Ignore conflicts – Allowing the system to prioritize the new plan.")

4. Toggle **Publish routes** to push plans to drivers immediately.

![Frame at 4:37](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_4_to_37.png "Publish toggle – Connecting drivers to their new routes.")

5. Opt to send automatic notifications to customers via email or SMS.

![Flow 5:04 to 5:21](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_5_to_04_to_5_to_21.gif "Notifications – Closing the communication loop with customers.")

6. Click **Next** to run the optimization.

![Frame at 5:55](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_5_to_55.png "Run optimization – Starting the background processing.")

### How To: Monitor and Review

1. Watch the **Progress bar** for live updates on team optimizations.

![Frame at 6:43](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_6_to_43.png "Monitoring – Tracking real-time progress.")

2. Click the **Open the route plan button** to analyze simulations.

![Frame at 6:58](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_6_to_58.png "Open plan button – Accessing detailed simulation results.")

3. Review routes in a **draft state** if publishing was left off.

![Frame at 7:36](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_7_to_36.png "Draft routes – Viewing routes held for manual review.")

4. Click on a route and manually publish it if necessary.

![Frame at 7:41](../images/Case-Studies-optimizing_missions_for_agency_timestamp_7_to_41.png "Manual publish – Finalizing routes that were not auto-published.")

#### Troubleshooting

*   If an error icon appears, click the **Open the route plan button**.
*   Click the **Warning button** inside the simulation to see the error reason.
*   Identify conflicts like incompatible time windows or vehicle constraint mismatches.

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Auto-Pull Past Missions**: The system automatically includes missions with delivery dates earlier than today.
*   💡 **Hands-Free Communication**: Enabling route publishing can trigger automatic customer notifications via SMS and email.
*   ⚠️ **Data Conflicts**: Do not close errors without investigating them as unresolved conflicts may hinder field execution.
