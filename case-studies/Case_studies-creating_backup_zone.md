# Case_studies-creating_backup_zone
# Case-Studies

The Backup Zone feature allows you to handle emergency subcontractor no-shows by creating temporary secondary sectors. It enables quick hand-offs of existing missions to available teams without changing your primary infrastructure. This ensures your SLAs are met and deliveries arrive on time despite overnight changes.

### Getting Started

*   Access to the **configuration module**.
*   An existing primary subzone requiring backup.
*   A replacement subcontractor available for assignment.

1. Navigate to the **configuration module**.

![Frame at 2:07](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_07.png "Configuration Module – Navigate to the configuration settings area.")

2. Click **manage studies and zones**.

![Frame at 2:09](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_09.png "Manage Studies and Zones – Open the study management list.")

3. Select the **pencil icon** next to the relevant study.

![Frame at 2:17](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_17.png "Pencil Icon – Click the edit button for the specific study.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Primary sub-sector**: The original delivery territory assigned to the first subcontractor.

![Frame at 2:42](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_42.png "Primary Sub-Sector – The current active assignment mode for the zone.")

*   **Secondary sub-sector**: The newly created backup zone assignment mode distinguishing it from the original.

![Frame at 3:37](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_3_to_37.png "Secondary Sub-Sector – The identification mode for the temporary backup zone.")

*   **Identifier**: A unique code field for the backup zone.

![Frame at 2:59](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_59.png "Identifier – Field used to name and track the backup zone.")

*   **Zone table**: The list within the study displaying both primary and backup zones.

![Frame at 3:26](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_3_to_36.png "Zone Table – List view showing all zones within the study.")

### How To: Creating a Backup Zone

1. Click the **primary subzone** you want to back up.

![Flow 2:33 to 2:42](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_33_to_2_to_42.gif "Primary Subzone Selection – Select the subzone to initiate the backup process.")

2. Open the **actions menu**.

![Frame at 2:50](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_50.png "Actions Menu – Open the list of available actions for the subzone.")

3. Select **create a backup zone**.

![Frame at 2:50](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_50.png "Create a Backup Zone – Select the option to clone the zone.")

4. Enter a new **identifier** and **name**.

![Flow 2:59 to 3:17](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_2_to_59_to_3_to_17.gif "Name and Identifier – Enter unique identification details for the clone.")

5. Click **OK** to create the zone.

![Frame at 3:17](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_3_to_17.png "OK Button – Confirm the creation of the backup zone.")

### How To: Assigning a Backup Subcontractor

1. Open the **actions menu** from the **study table**.

![Frame at 3:55](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_3_to_55.png "Study Table Actions – Access assignment options from the main table.")

2. Click **assign**.

![Frame at 3:59](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_3_to_59.png "Assign – Open the assignment configuration window.")

3. Choose the relevant study.

![Frame at 4:08](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_08.png "Select Study – Pick the study containing the backup zone.")

4. Click **actions** and select **assign** again.

![Frame at 4:09](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_09.png "Assign Action – Initiate the specific subcontractor selection.")

5. Pick a different subcontractor and driver for the backup zone.

![Flow 4:23 to 4:28](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_23_to_4_to_28.gif "Subcontractor Selection – Select the replacement team and driver.")

6. Click **assign** to link the territory.

![Frame at 4:29](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_29.png "Assign – Finalize the link between subcontractor and zone.")

### How To: Verifying the Assignment

1. Navigate to the **subcontractors module**.

![Frame at 4:51](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_51.png "Subcontractors Module – Access the subcontractor management area.")

2. Click **edit** on the replacement subcontractor.

![Frame at 4:58](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_4_to_58.png "Edit Subcontractor – Open the subcontractor profile for verification.")

3. Check the **secondary zones** drop-down.

![Frame at 5:06](../images/Case-Studies-Creating_Backup_Zone_timestamp_5_to_06.png "Secondary Zones – Verify the backup zone is listed in the profile.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Automatic Synchronization**: System updates happen immediately without manual refreshes after clicking assign.
*   ⚠️ **Existing Missions Update**: Replacement subcontractors cannot see current missions until you update the sector and deliverer info.
*   💡 **Preserve Infrastructure**: Backup zones are temporary and do not change your primary subzone configurations.
