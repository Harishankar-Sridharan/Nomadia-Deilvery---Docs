# 01_StudyCreationWithPrimaryZone
# Case Studies

Studies and zones define how your delivery territories are structured and organized. Use this feature to manage seasonal coverage changes and ensure orders land in the correct geographic areas. You will achieve a logistics operation that reflects real-world ground coverage throughout the year.

### Getting Started

Prerequisites and initial setup:
*   Access to the **Nomadia Delivery** application banner.
*   Administrative permissions to manage user roles and rights.

1. Click the **Configuration module** in the application banner.
![Frame at 1:51](../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_1_to_51.png "Configuration Module – The main entry point for system settings.")
2. Go to the **Manage users** page.
3. Edit the specific user and select the **Roles and rights** tab.
![Frame at 2:16](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_2_to_16.png "Roles and Rights Tab – Where user permissions for modules are assigned.")
4. Enable the rights for **List of zones**, **Assign zones**, and **Create and update zones**.
5. Enable **Access to the sectorization tool**, **Delete zones**, and **Delete studies**.
6. Click the **Save** button.
![Flow 2:16 to 3:00](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_2_to_16_to_3_to_00.gif "Permissions Setup – A sequence showing enabling and saving user rights.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Study Management page**: Access your central hub for creating and editing logistics territories.
![Frame at 3:21](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_3_to_21.png "Study Management Page – The central dashboard for all study and zone data.")
*   **Actions menu**: Open this menu to create new studies or add geographic zones.
![Frame at 3:36](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_3_to_36.png "Actions Menu – The dropdown containing creation and import tools.")
*   **Validity dates**: Set the overall start and end dates for a study's lifespan.
*   **Activation windows**: Configure specific seasonal time periods for the study to be active within a year.
*   **Assignment mode**: Define if a zone is a top-level **Primary zone** or a subzone.
![Frame at 6:26](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_6_to_26.png "Assignment Mode – The field used to designate the zone hierarchy level.")
*   **Map view**: Visualize postal code polygons automatically on the right side of the screen.
![Frame at 7:24](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_7_to_24.png "Map View – The visual representation of the defined geographic zones.")

### How To: Create a Study

1. Click the **Configuration module** and select the **Studies and zones** page.
![Frame at 3:15](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_3_to_15.png "Studies and Zones Selection – Navigating to the study management area.")
2. Click the **Actions menu** and select **Create empty study**.
![Flow 3:29 to 3:37](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_3_to_29_to_3_to_37.gif "Create Empty Study – The process of opening the new study form.")
3. Enter the **Identifier**, **Name**, and **Agency**.
4. Set the **Validity start date** and **Validity end date**.
5. Toggle the **Enable** switch to activate the study.
6. Select the specific days of the week for study activity.
7. Configure the seasonal activation period dates.
8. Click the **Save** button.
![Frame at 5:00](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_5_to_00.png "Save Study – Finalizing the study configuration parameters.")

### How To: Add a Primary Zone

1. Open your new study and switch to the **Zone tab**.
![Frame at 5:38](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_5_to_38.png "Zone Tab – Switching to the zone configuration view within a study.")
2. Click the **Actions menu** and select **Add a postal code zone**.
3. Fill in the **Identifier**, **Name**, and **Agency**.
4. Select the **Country** and verify the **Postal code normalized length**.
5. Select **Primary zone** in the **Assignment mode** field.
![Frame at 6:33](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_6_to_33.png "Primary Zone Selection – Setting the zone as a top-level territory.")
6. Enter postal codes manually or use **Import** for bulk uploads.
![Flow 6:41 to 7:07](../../images/Case-Studies-Study-creation-with-Primary-Zone_timestamp_6_to_41_to_7_to_07.gif "Postal Code Entry – Methods for adding geographic coverage to the zone.")
7. Click the **Save** button to go live.

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Bulk Uploads**: Use the **Import** option to save significant time when setting up entire regions.
*   💡 **Automated Mapping**: View your zones instantly as the platform renders polygons from postal codes automatically.
*   ⚠️ **Critical Step**: Ensure you select **Primary zone** in the **Assignment mode** field to define it as a top-level area.
