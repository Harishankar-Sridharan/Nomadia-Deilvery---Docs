# Case_studies-assigning_sub_zones
# # Case-Studies

Transform map shapes into operational territories by assigning them to specific teams and deliverers. This process ensures every delivery stop has a clear owner and synchronizes data across the entire platform. By completing these assignments, you make the delivery zones live and ready for daily operations.

### Getting Started

*   Ensure subzones are created and geographically balanced.
*   Confirm all internal and subcontractor deliverers are registered in the system.

1.  Navigate to the **study table**.
    ![Frame at 0:54](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_0_to_54.png "Study table – The main list where created studies and their subzones are viewed.")
2.  Select the specific study to assign.
    ![Flow 0:58 to 1:04](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_0_to_58_to_1_to_04.gif "Study Selection – Selecting the desired study from the list to begin assignment.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Actions menu**: Access the core assignment tools for your selected study.
    ![Frame at 1:04](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_04.png "Actions menu – The dropdown menu containing the assign option.")
*   **Assignment page**: The workspace where you define ownership for every individual subzone row.
    ![Frame at 1:10](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_10.png "Assignment page – A structured view showing subzone names and color codes.")
*   **Team dropdown**: Choose between an in-house team or a subcontractor team for a specific territory.
    ![Frame at 1:33](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_33.png "Team dropdown – Selection menu used to decide operational responsibility for a zone.")
*   **Deliverer dropdown**: Link a specific individual on the ground to a geographical boundary.
    ![Frame at 1:40](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_40.png "Deliverer dropdown – Selection menu for the specific person operating the territory.")

### How To: Assign Subzones and Verify Synchronization

1.  Open the **Actions menu** and click **assign**.
    ![Flow 1:04 to 1:10](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_04_to_1_to_10.gif "Assign Action – Moving from the study list to the detailed assignment interface.")
2.  Identify each subzone by its unique identifier and **color code**.
3.  Select the **in-house team** or **subcontractor team** for every subzone row.
    ![Flow 1:51 to 2:02](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_1_to_51_to_2_to_02.gif "Team Selection – Assigning operational responsibility to internal or external teams.")
4.  Pick the correct individual from the **Deliverer** dropdown for each row.
    ![Flow 2:05 to 2:31](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_2_to_05_to_2_to_31.gif "Deliverer Selection – Linking agents to their specific geographical territories.")
5.  Click the **assign button** to finalize the ownership.
    ![Frame at 3:41](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_3_to_41.png "Assign button – The final click to formally link subzones to their owners.")
6.  Navigate to the **configuration module** and select the **manage users page** to verify.
    ![Flow 4:08 to 4:24](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_4_to_08_to_4_to_24.gif "User Management – Navigating to check if assignments reached the deliverer profile.")
7.  Click **edit** on the deliverer and view the **agencies and zones tab**.
    ![Frame at 4:31](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_4_to_31.png "Agencies and zones tab – Viewing the live synchronized assignment and map on the user record.")
8.  Check the **subcontractor module** to confirm the assignment is reflected in their **profile**.
    ![Flow 4:59 to 5:11](../images/Case-Studies-assigning_sub_zones_timestamp_4_to_59_to_5_to_11.gif "Subcontractor Profile – Confirming data flows automatically to external partner records.")

### Productivity Tips

- 💡 **Delegated Assignment**: Grant subcontractors rights to assign subzones so they can manage their own internal deliverers.
- 💡 **Instant Sync**: System data flows automatically across the platform without needing a manual refresh or sync.
- ⚠️ **Unassigned Zones**: Avoid leaving subzones unassigned as they remain non-operational shapes on the map without owners.
