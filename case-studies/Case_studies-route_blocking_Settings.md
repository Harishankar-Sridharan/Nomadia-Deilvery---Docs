# Case-Studies

Route blocking systematically prevents drivers from starting routes containing high-value items until a planner reviews them. This feature minimizes financial risks like cargo theft and ensures security protocols are followed every day. Users achieve a proactive, documented compliance process for every delivery.

### Getting Started

Requirements:
* Missions must include a **package value** field.
* Access to the **configuration module** is required.

Steps:
1. Open the **configuration module**.

![Frame at 2:21](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_2_to_21.png "Configuration module – Accessing the system settings.")

2. Select **settings** under the **route** section.

![Frame at 2:25](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_2_to_25.png "Route settings – Navigating to blocking configurations.")

3. Enter a numerical threshold in the **package value condition** field.

![Frame at 2:51](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_2_to_51.png "Package value condition – Defining the monetary limit for automatic blocking.")

4. Click **save** to apply the configuration.

![Frame at 3:00](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_3_to_00.png "Save button – Finalizing the configuration setup.")

### Feature Overview

* **Package value condition**: Define the monetary threshold that triggers an automatic route lock.

![Frame at 2:30](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_2_to_30.png "Package value condition – Field used to set the value threshold.")

* **Lock symbol**: This icon appearing on a route signifies it contains a high-value mission and is blocked.

![Frame at 4:44](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_4_to_44.png "Lock symbol – Visual indicator that a route is currently blocked.")

* **Tooltip**: Hover over the lock to see the specific reason for the block.

![Frame at 4:51](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_4_to_51.png "Tooltip – Explanation of why the departure is not allowed.")

* **Departure allowed drop-down**: Select between **No blocking**, **Blocked**, or **Granted** to manage route access.

![Frame at 5:32](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_5_to_32.png "Departure allowed drop-down – Menu for updating the route status.")

* **Log section**: Review the history of who approved a route and when it occurred for compliance.

![Frame at 7:09](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_7_to_09.png "Log section – Audit trail for security and accountability.")

### How To: Optimize and Identify Blocked Routes

1. Navigate to the **mission tab**.

![Frame at 3:20](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_3_to_20.png "Mission tab – Viewing the list of missions to optimize.")

2. Select missions that exceed your configured **package value**.

![Flow 3:28 to 3:46](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_3_to_28_to_3_to_46.gif "Mission selection – Highlighting high-value items for route creation.")

3. Click **optimize**.

![Frame at 3:55](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_3_to_55.png "Optimize button – Triggering the route calculation.")

4. Select the new route and click **validate**.

![Flow 4:21 to 4:30](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_4_to_21_to_4_to_30.gif "Route validation – Finalizing the optimized route for the driver.")

5. Look for the **lock symbol** in the **mission tab** to confirm the block is active.

![Frame at 4:40](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_4_to_40.png "Mission tab – Identifying blocked routes via the lock icon.")

### How To: Grant Access to a Blocked Route

1. Click the **pencil icon** next to the blocked route.

![Frame at 5:25](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_5_to_25.png "Pencil icon – Editing the details of a blocked route.")

2. Open the **departure allowed drop-down**.

![Frame at 6:50](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_6_to_50.png "Departure allowed drop-down – Accessing status options.")

3. Select **granted**.

![Frame at 6:55](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_6_to_55.png "Granted option – Approving the route for departure.")

4. Click the **save button**.

![Frame at 6:58](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_6_to_58.png "Save button – Confirming the status change.")

5. Verify the **green unlocked icon** is now displayed.

![Frame at 7:02](../images/Case-Studies-Route_Blocking_Settings_timestamp_7_to_02.png "Green unlocked icon – Confirmation that the driver can now start the route.")

### Productivity Tips

* 💡 **Quick Filtering**: Filter by **departure allowed** in the mission tab to instantly see all blocked routes.
* 💡 **Compliance Records**: Use the **log section** to document who reviewed high-value missions for future disputes.
* ⚠️ **Automatic Lock**: Drivers cannot start routes in the mobile app if the status is **Blocked**.
