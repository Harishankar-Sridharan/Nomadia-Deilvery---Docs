# Case_studies-Merging_Sub_Zones
# Case-Studies

Merging sub-zones recombines divided territories once a demand surge has passed. This feature helps you maintain a lean zone structure and ensures efficient driver routes. By merging, you reduce planning overhead and return to a simplified one-driver, one-territory configuration.

### Getting Started

*   Ensure you have access to the **Configuration** module.
*   Identify the **Primary Zone** containing the sub-zones you want to merge.
*   The **Primary Zone** must have the assignment mode set to **primary sector**.

1.  Click on the **Configuration** module in the main navigation.

![Frame at 1:57](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_1_to_57.png "Configuration – Accessing the system settings and module selection.")

2.  Select **Manage zones and studies** from the menu.

![Frame at 1:58](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_1_to_58.png "Manage zones and studies – Opening the zone management dashboard.")

3.  Click **Edit** on the specific study containing your sub-zones.

![Frame at 2:01](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_01.png "Edit Study – Entering the zone editing environment.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Primary Zone**: The high-level territory required to view and manage all contained sub-zones simultaneously.
![Frame at 2:03](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_03.png "Primary Zone – Selection of the parent territory for merging.")

*   **Subsectorize**: The action button used to launch the **Territory Manager** for the selected zone.
![Frame at 2:09](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_09.png "Subsectorize – Button to initiate territorial realignment.")

*   **Territory Manager**: A workspace showing the map and zone table for active modifications.
![Frame at 2:23](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_23.png "Territory Manager – Full map view of all sub-zones within the primary zone.")

*   **Merge Territories**: The command that combines two selected sub-zones into a single contiguous geography.
![Frame at 3:04](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_04.png "Merge Territories – Action to join selected sub-zones.")

*   **Identifier**: The unique name or label assigned to the newly merged sub-zone.
![Frame at 3:12](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_12.png "Identifier Selection – Choosing the permanent name for the merged zone.")

### How To: Merge Sub-Zones

1.  Navigate to the **Zones** tab.

2.  Select the **Primary Zone**.

![Frame at 2:05](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_05.png "Zones Tab – Selecting the primary level for merging.")

3.  Click the **Action** button and select **Subsectorize**.

![Frame at 2:08](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_08.png "Action Menu – Selecting subsectorize to open the map.")

4.  Enter your **Pre-filter** values for agency, period, and relevant days.

![Frame at 2:14](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_14.png "Pre-filter – Entering data filters to match mission requirements.")

5.  Click **Assign** to open the map view.

![Frame at 2:17](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_17.png "Assign – Loading the territory manager interface.")

6.  In the table next to the map, select the two sub-zones you want to merge.

![Frame at 2:57](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_2_to_57.png "Table Selection – Highlighting the two territories to be combined.")

7.  Click **Actions** and select **Merge territories**.

![Flow 3:00 to 3:04](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_00_to_3_to_04.gif "Merge Flow – Selecting the merge action from the table menu.")

8.  Select the **Identifier** you wish to keep for the new zone.

![Frame at 3:10](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_10.png "Select Identifier – Picking the identifier for the unified zone.")

9.  Click **Merge**.

![Frame at 3:16](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_16.png "Merge Confirmation – Finalizing the combination of zones.")

10. Review the new unified geography on the map.

![Frame at 3:38](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_38.png "Map Review – Confirming the boundary line has been removed.")

11. Click **Validate the sectorization** in the top right corner.

![Frame at 3:55](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_3_to_55.png "Validate – Submitting the new zone layout for review.")

12. Click **Save** on the review page to update the zone table.

![Frame at 4:09](../images/Case-Studies-Merging_Sub_Zones_timestamp_4_to_09.png "Save – Permanently applying changes to the study.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Operational Efficiency**: Recombine zones as soon as demand normalizes to avoid half-empty routes.
*   💡 **Consistent Naming**: Choose the identifier your team is most familiar with when prompted during a merge.
*   ⚠️ **Selection Level**: Never select a sub-zone level to start a merge; the merge option will not appear.
