# 04_SubSectorUsingPostalCode
# User Guide: Automated Subsectoring via Direct Postal Code Mapping

This guide will help you quickly turn your primary delivery zones into detailed subzones using the postal codes you already have. This method is the fastest way to organize your territories when postal codes are already very precise.

---

### 1. What is Postal Code Subsectoring?
In many areas, postal codes are so detailed that they already represent the perfect delivery territory. Instead of using complex tools to balance workloads or mission data, this feature allows you to turn **one postal code into one subzone** instantly.

**Key Benefits:**
*   **Instant Results**: No waiting for processing or "balancing" calculations.
*   **Precision**: Uses the exact, official boundaries of the postal codes.
*   **Simplicity**: One click creates all your subzones at once.

💡 **Real-World Example**: If you have a primary zone for a city that includes 15 different postal codes, using this tool will automatically create 15 individual subzones—one for each code—ready for your delivery team.

---

### 2. How to Subsector Your Zone
Follow these simple steps to transform your primary zone.

**Step 1: Navigate to Your Study**
1.  Open the **Configuration** module in the platform.
2.  Select **Manage Studies and Zones**.

![GIF at 1:42–1:54](../../images/04_SubSectorUsingPostalCode_timestamp_1_to_42–1_to_54.gif "Opening Configuration and Editing a Study – Study Management View")


**Step 2: Select Your Primary Zone**
1.  Once inside the study, click on the **Zone** tab at the top.
2.  Click on the **Primary Zone** you wish to break down.

![GIF at 1:54–2:01](../../images/04_SubSectorUsingPostalCode_timestamp_1_to_54–2_to_01.gif "Navigating to the Zone tab and selecting a zone – Zone Table List")


**Step 3: Run the Automatic Subsectoring**
1.  With your zone selected, click to open the **Actions** menu.
2.  Look for the option labeled **Subsectorize based on zip codes** and click it.

![GIF at 2:23–2:27](../../images/04_SubSectorUsingPostalCode_timestamp_2_to_23–2_to_27.gif "Selecting 'Subsectorize based on zip codes' – Actions Dropdown Menu")

    *   📹 Timestamp 2:27–2:39 (04_SubSectorUsingPostalCode.mp4): Clicking the option – Subzones appearing instantly in the table

### 4. Productivity Tips
*   💡 **Choose the Right Tool**: Use the **Territory Manager** if you need to balance workloads based on mission numbers. Use **Postal Code Mapping** if your geography is already naturally divided by zip codes.
*   💡 **Save Time**: This feature eliminates the need for "wizards" or pop-up menus, making it the fastest path to a structured zone.
*   💡 **What's Next?**: Once your subzones are built, the platform can automatically assign new delivery missions to the correct person based on these territories.
